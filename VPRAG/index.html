BRIEF

Chandrayaan-3


Chandrayaan-3 is the third Indian lunar exploration mission under the Indian Space Research Organisation's (ISRO) Chandrayaan programme. It consists of a lander named Vikram and a rover named Pragyan, similar to those of the Chandrayaan-2 mission. The mission was launched on July 14, 2023, from the Satish Dhawan Space Centre in Sriharikota, Andhra Pradesh. It successfully entered lunar orbit on August 1, 2023, and soft-landed on the lunar surface on August 23, 2023. The lander Vikram touched down on the lunar surface at the South Pole, making India the first country to successfully land a spacecraft on the lunar south pole. The rover Pragyan is currently exploring the lunar surface in the vicinity of the lander.
